More Details About Our Companies

 

  • Green Way Trading and Contracting

    In 1978, and over 12 years, Green Way Trading and Contracting Company built and constructed more than 25 buildings and residential towers, 21 of which are in Cairo, most of them in Heliopolis and 4 in Alexandria, in Mandara, El Asafra and Khaled Ibn El Walid Street.

  • Agricultural and Animal Production Company

    In 1982, on an area of 31 acres and 8 carats, on the Belbeis Desert Road, the activity of Agricultural and Animal Production Company started by building egg wards, producing 15 million eggs annually, gradually until April 1984. Production reached 60 million of eggs in the period in which a livestock and sheep farm was built.

  • Food security

    In 1986, a ward for raising rabbits with a capacity of 500 mms was established, as well as cultivating an area of about 30 acres to produce olives, figs and palms with the latest types of irrigation for each type of planting, whether drip irrigation or digging about 8 wells in separate places of the land.

  • Food Refrigeration

    In 1997, a refrigerator for refrigeration and freezing was built, which consisted of 12 cold stores and 4 freezer stores operating at f capacity to cool and store agricultural products and crops and factories for major companies in Egypt

  • Medico Company for Import and Export

    In 1994, Medico Company or Import and Export was established as a sister company of the group of companies headed by the engineer. Mohamed Ahmed Helmy Shaker, who had been chaired by the group's presidency from 2011, which from this date became the chairman of the group's board of directors, which was since its inception and the supply of ammonia tanks for the Ministry of Agriculture, as well as import and supply of spare parts for special ceramic plant machinery in Egypt through the Italian company Siti B&T.

  • Intensive Olive Cultivation Company

    On 1/1/2020, in the field of increasing the agricultural area of the policy and directives of the President of the one and a half acres project, we purchased 1225 acres in the Siwa Oasis from the Egyptian countryside company specialized in selling state lands and implementing this project to cultivate them with several varieties of olives intended for the production of the finest olive oil using the latest agricultural systems The condensed as well as the palm trees and the olive presses and purification for the purpose of exporting abroad and the production and packaging of dates with the finest types for export.
